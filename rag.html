A technique that enhances AI responses by retrieving relevant information from external knowledge bases before generating answers.

  • Augments LLM context with retrieved information
  • Focuses on knowledge retrieval and integration
  • Primarily handles static or semi-static data

A standardized protocol that enables AI models to securely connect to and interact with external systems, tools, and services.

  • Connects models to live systems and APIs
  • Enables real-time data access and actions
  • Supports both reading and writing operations
  • Provides standardized tool integration
  • Works with dynamic, real-time data sources
  • Enables complex workflows and automations
